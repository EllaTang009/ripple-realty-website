Our Story

Built where technology and real estate intersect

Ripple Realty Group was founded on a simple observation: the way people buy, sell, and build homes has changed, but most brokerages haven't. We built a firm that pairs the standards of a premium advisory with the tools of a modern technology company — and extended it with true construction and development capability.
